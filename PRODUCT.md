# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Orbis serves internal partner success teams and external partners working inside a shared platform. Internal users manage partner enablement, content, leads, and configuration. External partner users need a calm, efficient place to learn, find resources, review leads, and track their progress without friction.

## Product Purpose

Orbis is a premium partner success platform. Its job is to give organizations a clear operating surface for partner enablement while giving partners a polished self-serve experience. Success for this product means fast orientation, clear permissions, low UI noise, and a structure that can scale into a larger multi-role platform without rework.

## Brand Personality

Professional, calm, premium.

The product should feel modern and fast, but never loud. It should signal trust, operational maturity, and clarity. Users should feel like they are working in a focused system rather than a generic admin starter.

## Anti-references

- No decorative startup-dashboard clutter
- No marketing-style hero treatment inside authenticated product screens
- No hyper-saturated SaaS gradients or purple-heavy AI aesthetics
- No oversized cards, noisy shadows, or ornamental motion

## Design Principles

- Keep task surfaces quiet so information reads immediately.
- Separate partner and admin concerns at the layout boundary, not through ad hoc conditionals.
- Make permissions explicit in code and predictable in the UI.
- Prefer reusable, typed building blocks over page-specific markup.
- Let premium quality come from spacing, hierarchy, and restraint rather than decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible navigation. Use restrained motion only where it conveys state. Keep color roles semantic and avoid depending on color alone for meaning. Use compact but readable typography appropriate for product UI.
